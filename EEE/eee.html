Electrical and Electronics Engineering




Vision

To develop the Dept. of Electrical and Electronics as a widely acknowledged centre in academics and research to meet the ever-changing needs of the country, industry and society.

Mission

All-round personality development of the students to meet the demands of emerging technologies in the field of electrical and electronics and excel professionally through quality technical education. To develop creativity and research temperament in students to reach greater heights of professional ethics and social responsibility.

About the department

       Dr. A. S. Jadhav
      Head of the department
The department was established in 1980 to provide state-of-the-art education and training in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. M.Tech. in Microelectronics and Control Systems was started in 2013 with an intake of 18. The department offers B.E. in Electrical and Electronics (four years) with an intake of 120 and an M.Tech. in Microelectronics and Control Systems (two years)
