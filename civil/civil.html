Vision

To emerge as a premier department in technical education and research to meet the infrastructural needs and challenges of society

Mission

To impart technical education to students by adopting innovative teaching/learning processes and honing their soft skills to help them build successful careers. To develop in students an aptitude for innovation, leadership and solving social issues ethically through the curriculum, which is reinforced with co and extra-curricular activities
