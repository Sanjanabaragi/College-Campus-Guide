Vision

Students focus on building a strong foundation in core subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry, which are essential across all fields of engineering.

Mission

To enhance the potential of first-year engineering students by providing a strong technical education foundation and practical training across core engineering disciplines. This mission aims to instill professional ethics, foster critical thinking, and cultivate problem-solving abilities. Through hands-on learning, exposure to various engineering domains, and a focus on innovation, students are encouraged to explore new ideas and entrepreneurial opportunities. The mission prioritizes the holistic development of students, preparing them to contribute meaningfully to society while promoting integrity, teamwork, and ethical practices in their professional journey.
